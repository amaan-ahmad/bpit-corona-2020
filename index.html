ABOUT CORONA

We are glad to inform you that BHAGWAN PARSHURAM INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY is organizing their annual cultural-techno management festival "CORONA'20" on 26th-27th March 2020 with the dream of spreading its wings and expanding its reach beyond previously unseen levels of events. Over the years the festival has attracted students from B. TECH, BBA, BCA, MBA and various other streams to showcase their creative skills and compete amongst the best from the educational institutes of Delhi and NCR Region. CORONA is a festival comprising of a big chain of events under following categories: - CULTURAL EVENTS Mr & Ms. CORONA: A beauty pageant event for boys and girls. It is a crowd boost event with the participation of all excelled students. Street Play: Different college teams narrate express fully on general topics. Fashion Parade: It is a grandeur event of different colleges and fashion institutes to showcase the upcoming line of fashion clothing and debut every season. Freeze The Floor: Dance is another name of soul satisfaction. It gives a beautiful and glorious platform to the human being to portray feelings in the form of expressions. Solo Singing: The Rhythmic act of producing musical sounds with the voice and augments tonality. Battle of Bands: Vocalists betting out the words, Drummers with their Grooves & Rhythms, Guitarists taking the convenient option either melting your face or touching your souls with their solos, BAND competing amongst themselves for supremacy. TECHNICAL EVENTS LFR (Line following robot), LAN Gaming, PUBG Mobile, Robo Soccer, Robo Race, Techno Treasure hunt, Datathon, Ideathon, TUG of Bots. MANAGEMENT EVENTS B-Plan, Quiz, JAM, Big Idea Market and several more events. Further categories of dance events are: Solo Dance Group, Dance Street.
